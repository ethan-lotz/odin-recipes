Cereal and Milk

Description

Do you love a nice cold bowl of cereal, but find yourself stymied at every turn when you attempt to craft this delicious dish? Do you spend thousands monthly on a fleet of servants to pour your tasty breakfast treats? Well fret no more, because this recipe is a one-way ticket to the best bowl you've ever had.

Ingredients

  • Cereal (one box)
  • Milk (one half gallon)

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into a large bowl until full
  2. Slowly mix in the wet ingredients until the cereal begins to rise
  3. Enjoy with a spoon or hands
  4. Repeat until cereal box is empty

Tips

  • Leftover milk? Try drinking it straight!
  • No bowls? Any container will do. In a pinch, pour ingredients directly into mouth.
  • For a fun twist, try replacing the cereal with metal shavings or forgotten dreams.
